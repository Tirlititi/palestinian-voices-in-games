Quasar
Tales from the Endless Void

By Palestinian developer and designer Arcane Whiskers, Quasar, Tales from the Endless Void is a tabletop role playing game. While still in development, the current version of the game is already available and can be played either as a narrative driven system with a Game Master, or as a co-op skirmish game where enemy characters move and act based on easy to follow decision trees and an aggro system.

The setting for Quasar takes place In the year 2430 within the third quadrant of the Orion Cygnus arm of the Milky way galaxy. Here powerful factions and their opposing ideologies threaten to plunge the quadrant into all-out war, ultimately it is a place where brave individuals can face against overwhelming odds to shed some light on an increasingly dark and unfair existence.
